ABOUT ME

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, in the group of Anna Wienhard , mentored by James Farre . I did my PhD at Sorbonne Université, in Paris, under the supervision of Bram Petri.

Before that, I was in Barcelona -at the UB-, where I did my bachelor and master, under the supervision of Xavier Massaneda and Joaquím Ortega.

I'm originally from Ibiza (Spain).

My research interests lie in low dimensional geometry and topology, hyperbolic geometry and random geometry. I'm also interested in the combinatorics of triangulations of manifolds and its related graphs. I'm currently working on random hyperbolic 3-manifolds.
