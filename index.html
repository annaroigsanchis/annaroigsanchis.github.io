ABOUT ME

I'm an associate professor (maîtresse de conférences) at Université Côte d'Azur, in Nice, since September 2025.

Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, in the group of Anna Wienhard , mentored by James Farre . I did my PhD at Sorbonne Université (IMJ-PRG) , in Paris, under the supervision of Bram Petri.

I'm originally from Ibiza (Spain).

My research interests lie in low dimensional geometry and topology, hyperbolic geometry and random geometry. I'm also interested in the combinatorics of triangulations of manifolds and its related graphs. I'm currently working on random hyperbolic 3-manifolds.

Outside mathematics, I'm a typical person; I like spending my time with good company, having good food, in a good weather and with some good music (whatever that means).
